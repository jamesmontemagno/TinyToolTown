Ghostwriter App is an AI-assisted content creation tool that guides users through a two-step process: an adaptive interview to gather ideas, followed by automated article generation. It supports multiple AI models, real-time streaming, and custom voice/style guides for tailored results. Built with Astro and Tailwind CSS, Ghostwriter offers a modern, persistent writing experience for technical, narrative, educational, or review content.
