GuckGuck is your open-source sidekick for periodic snapshots of your screen which you can view online. Need to monitor a task on your machine over time without sitting in front of your screen? GuckGuck periodically captures snapshots and uploads them to the GuckGuck web portal, where you can view and download them online.
