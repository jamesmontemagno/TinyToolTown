Learn with Nico’s AI-900 Audio Player is a lightweight, no-nonsense study companion for Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals. More to come like AZ 900 and other, as It turns a big question bank (1,058 items) into an audio-first “press play and grind” experience. 

You can filter by exam section, switch between sequential/random, adjust playback speed, bookmark questions, and track progress so you can pick up where you left off. 

I built it to make studying easier for busy people who learn better by listening, and to keep the whole experience fast, simple, and kind of fun.
