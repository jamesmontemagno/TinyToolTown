Pong’ish is a browser-first arcade game that takes the classic and legendary Pong and adds modern momentum mechanics like timed power-ups and shield saves. I built it cuz, why not.  It’s instantly playable, fast to rematch, and constantly surprising through dynamic moments, responsive controls, and punchy A/V feedback.
