Retake is a native macOS screen recorder that lets you punch in and re-record any section on the fly — no post-production editing required. Messed up a demo? Hit Redo, scrub back to the right moment, and keep going. It was built out of frustration with re-recording entire screencasts just to fix a 10-second flub. With redaction, trim, and pause built right into the recording flow, you go from capture to a polished video in one pass — no timeline editor, no export queue, no fiddling.
