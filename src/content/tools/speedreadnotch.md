SpeedReadNotch is a native macOS application that helps you read faster and more efficiently by displaying text word-by-word directly in your MacBook's notch. Using the Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP) technique, it eliminates subvocalization and eye backtracking while maintaining comprehension. With customizable settings and global shortcuts, SpeedReadNotch makes speed reading accessible and unobtrusive.
