Stash is a lightweight macOS menu bar app for instantly capturing tasks, questions, goals, and reminders with a global shortcut (Cmd+Shift+Space). Built to remove capture friction and make "quickly save this before I forget" feel effortless. Keyboard-first flow, clean minimal UI, local-first storage, and AI-powered reminder parsing that understands natural language.
